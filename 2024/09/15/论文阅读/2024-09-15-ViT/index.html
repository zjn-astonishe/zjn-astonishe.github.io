AN IMAGE IS WORTH 16X16 WORDS: TRANSFORMERS FOR IMAGE RECOGNITION AT SCALE

Abstract

Applications to computer vision remain limited

  • attention relys on CNNs

    • is either applied in conjunction with covolutional networks(结合卷积网络),
    • or used to replace certain components of convolutional networks(替换卷积网络组件)
    • keeping convolutional networks overall structure in place(卷积网络是完整的)

  • article shows that this reliance on CNNs is not necessary

    • Conclusion: a pure transformer applied directly to sequences of image patches can perform very well on image classification tasks(纯净的Transformer也可直接应用于处理图像补丁序列的分类任务)
    • Action: Pre-trained on large amounts of data and then transferred to multiple mid-sized or small image recognition benchmarks(ImageNet, CIFAR-100, VTAB). (将用大量数据预训练好的模型迁移到多个中小型的图像识别预测中)
    • Results: Vision Transformer(ViT) attains excellent results compared to state-of-the-art convolutional networks while require sub-stantially fewer computational resources to train(Transformer用于视觉与最先进的卷积网络相比，有更出色的结果，更少的计算资源)

Introduction

The use of Transformers

  • Self-attention-based architecutres, have become the model of choice in natural language processing.
  • The dominant approach is to pre-train on a large text corpus and then fine-tune on a smaller task-specific dataset(在大型文本语料库进行预训练，训练完后在较小的特定任务数据集上进行微调)
  • Transformers’ computational efficiency and scalability, it has become possible to train models of unprecedented size, with over 100B parameters(Transformer的计算效率和可扩展性，可以训练超过100B个参数的空前规模大模型)
  • 甚至即使模型和数据集不断增长，Transformer的性能还没有出现饱和(saturating performance)的迹象

Related works

    • Prajit Ramachandran, Niki Parmar, Ashish Vaswani, Irwan Bello, Anselm Levskaya, and Jon Shlens. Stand-alone self-attention in vision models. In NeurIPS, 2019.
      • theoretically efficient, but have not yet been scaled effectively on modern hardware accelerators due to the use of specialized attention patterns(理论有效，但专门的注意力机制还没在现代硬件加速器上实现)

Main Work of this paper

  • apply a standard Transformer directly to images, with the fewest possible modifications.(尽可能少的修改，直接应用Transformer模型到图像)
    • split an image into patches and provide the sequence of linear embeddings of these patches as an input to a Transformer(将图像分割成块，将这些块通过嵌入层得到的序列作为Transformer的输入)
    • Image patches are treated the same way as tokens(words) in an NLP application(图像分割的块和NLP中的token地位相当)
    • train the model on image classification in supervised fashion(监督方式训练模型进行图像的分类)

The result of this paper

  • When trained on mid-sized datasets, models yield modest accuracies of a few percentage points below ResNets of comparable size. It do not generalize well when trained on insufficient amounts of data(数据不够的时候泛化地不够好，不如ResNets等模型)

    • ImageNet without strong regularization(因为这些数据集没有很强的正则化)
    • Transformers lack some of the inductive biases inherent to CNNs, such as translation equivariance and locality(对于CNNs来说，缺少固有的归纳偏置，例如权重共享确保的平移等价性和感受野受限确保的局部性)

  • the picture changes if the models are trained on larger dataset(14M-300M images). Vision Transformer(ViT) attains excellent results when pre-trained at sufficient scale and transferred to tasks with fewer data points(经过足够的数据进行预训练后，迁移到更少数据的任务时有更好的表现)

    • Large scale training trumps inductive bias

METHOD

  • follow the original Transformers as closely as possible. (尽量贴近原著)
    • An advantage of this intentionally simple setup(有意简单设置) is that scalable NLP Transformer architectures, and their efficient implementations can be used almout out of the box(架构和实现的可迁移性强).

VISION TRANSFORMER(ViT)

  • The standard Transformer receive as input a 1D sequence of token embeddings. (标准接收输入的是一维的嵌入tokens)
  • To handle 2D images, reshape the image $x \in R^{H\times W\times C}$ into a sequence of flattened 2D patches $x_p \in R^{N\times (P^2, C)}$, where $(H, W)$ is the resolution of the original image(原图的像素), $C$ is the number of channels(通道的数量，如颜色通道), $(P, P)$ is the resolution of each image patch(图像分片的像素), and $N = HW/P^2$ is the resulting number of patches(图像分片的数量), which also serves as the effective input sequence length for the Transformer. The Transformer uses constant latent vector size $D$ through all of its layers(使用大小为D的恒定潜在矢量), so flatten the patches(要将图像分片从二维展开为一维) and map to $D$ dimensions with a trainable linear projection, refer to the output of this projection as the patch embeddings(使用可训练的线性投影映射到D维度，这个投影就叫做分片的嵌入层).

  • split an image into fixed-size patches, linearly embed each of them, add position embeddings, and feed the resulting sequence of vectors to a standard Transformer encoder.
  • In order to perform classification, use the standard approach of adding an extra learnable “classification token” to the sequence.
